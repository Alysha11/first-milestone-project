About

We are The Monkees! Bringing music to you since 1966

Davy Jones

vocals, percussion, drums, guitar

Hailing from Manchester, England Jones was a singer-songwriter, musician, actor and businessman, best known as a member of the Monkees. Jones is considered one of the great teen idols.

Micky Dolenz

vocals, drums, percussion, guitar, moog synthesizer

Dolenz had his own rock group called "Micky and the One-Nighters" in the early mid-1960s. In 1965, he was cast in the sitcom The Monkees and became the drummer and a lead vocalist in the band.

Michael Nesmith

guitar, vocals, keyboards, harmonica

Nesmith from Houston Texas is a songwriter, actor, producer, novelist, businessman, and philanthropist. Nesmith's songwriting credits include "Different Drum" (sung by Linda Ronstadt with the Stone Poneys).

Peter Tork

bass guitar, vocals, guitar, keyboards, banjo

Although Tork was born in Columbia in 1942 many news articles incorrectly report him as born in 1944 in New York City, a date and place given on early Monkees press releases. Tork learnt to play many instruments, including the banjo and both acoustic bass and guitars.
